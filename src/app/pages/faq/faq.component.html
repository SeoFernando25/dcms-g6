Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

We get a lot of questions, naturally, about the wide range of dental services we perform here at our "name" dental clinics. Here are the answers to the most frequently asked questions. Don't see yours answered here? Feel free to reach out to us.

Q: What if I have a dental emergency?

A: Our dental clinics offer same-day appointments for Ontario residents. Whether you’re experiencing serious tooth pain, or you’ve chipped or broken a tooth, it’s important you get in touch with us as soon as you realize you need to make an emergency visit to the dentist.

Q: What sort of Covid-19 precautions can I expect from your clinic?

A: Currently, we are implementing all covid precautions mandated by the Government of Ontario. In addition to this, our clinic requires all staff to wear masks at all times. Our staff are also required to be vaccinated against Covid-19. If you feel that you might have Covid-19 symptoms, we ask that you call us to reschedule your appointment.

Q: Why are regular dental exams important?

A: Going to the dentist on a regular basis is very important to ensure that you maintain good dental health. Regular appointments can help spot issues early on and help you avoid even bigger treatments later on. We recommend that all of our patients visit us at least every 6 months in order to help protect your teeth.

Q: Do you accept walk-in patients without a scheduled appointment?

A: Yes! We are able to accommodate patients who do not have a scheduled appointment. If you would like to see what services we offer, please click here.

Q: My teeth seem fine. Should I still go to the dentist?

A: Even if your teeth feel fine, it is important to visit the dentist because problems could arise without you realizing. Many dental issues become worse over time. If these issues are not treated right away, they can lead to more severe issues later on.
