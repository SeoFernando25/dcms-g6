Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

We get a lot of questions, naturally, about the wide range of dental services we perform here at our "name" dental clinics. Here are the answers to the most frequently asked questions. Don't see yours answered here? Feel free to reach out to us.

Q: What if I have a dental emergency?

Our dental clinics offer same-day appointments for Ontario residents. Whether you’re experiencing serious tooth pain, or you’ve chipped or broken a tooth, it’s important you get in touch with us as soon as you realize you need to make an emergency visit to the dentist.
