Welcome To Our Clinic!

At Ontario Dental Care, we focus on preventative dental care. Our mission is to provide you with the highest quality care that is able to anticipate future problems and protect your dental health into your senior years.

We are also able to provide a wide range of services that include general, cosmetic and restorative dentistry. This includes everything from routine check-ups to surgeries. We hope to welcome you in as one of our patients in the future!

About Us

Founded in 2004, Ontario Dental Care has been providing dental care to our community for almost 2 decades. Dental care is a big passion of ours, and we are happy to be able to help those in our community. If you are looking for a new dentist, we hope that you would join us and become one of our patients!
